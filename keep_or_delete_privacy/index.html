Privacy Policy – Keep or Delete

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Keep or Delete mobile application ("App") handles information when you use our service. The App is designed to help you review and organize photos and videos stored on your device by allowing you to keep or delete them using simple swipe gestures.

Summary: Keep or Delete does not collect or transmit your photos, videos, or personal data to external servers. All processing happens locally on your device.

1. Personal Data Collection

Keep or Delete does not collect personal data such as name, email address, phone number, location data, or advertising identifiers.

2. Access to Media Files

In order to function properly, the App may request permission from your Android device to:

  • Access photos and videos stored on your device.
  • Delete media files selected by you after confirmation.

The App only accesses the media files and basic metadata required for its functionality (such as file date, type, or size when available). No photos or videos are uploaded or transmitted externally.

3. How We Use the Information

  • Organize your media by month.
  • Display previews of photos and videos.
  • Allow you to mark items to keep or delete.
  • Execute deletion only after your confirmation.

4. Local Storage

The App may store certain preferences locally on your device, such as:

  • Whether you have completed the tutorial.
  • Your temporary keep/delete decisions until confirmation.
  • Theme preference (light or dark mode).

This information remains stored only on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the App or clearing its data in Android system settings.

5. Data Sharing

Keep or Delete does not share your photos, videos, or personal data with third parties. The App does not use external servers to collect user data.

6. Third-Party Libraries

The App may use third-party libraries to manage permissions, access media files, or display video previews. These libraries operate locally on your device. The App does not integrate analytics or tracking services by default.

7. Android Deletion Confirmation

In newer Android versions, the system may require additional confirmation before deleting media files. This is a security feature enforced by Android. Keep or Delete respects these system protections and will only delete files after your explicit confirmation.

8. Security

The App is designed to process all data locally and minimize exposure. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your Android device updated and backing up your data regularly.

9. Children's Privacy

Keep or Delete is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
